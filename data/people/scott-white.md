Scott White was arrested on 12 May 2020 and charged with the murder of Scott Johnson,
following the investigation by Strike Force Welsford (established 2018 in the wake of
State Coroner Barnes' 2017 homicide finding).

He pleaded guilty to a charge of manslaughter in February 2023 and was sentenced in
June 2023. The sentencing judgment is *R v White* [2023] NSWSC 611, in which
Beech-Jones CJ at CL described the suggestion that Scott Johnson died by suicide as
"an absurd suggestion."

The original charge was murder. No details of the defense raised, or of his sentence,
appear in the Sackar Inquiry report. Further details are in the sentencing judgment.
