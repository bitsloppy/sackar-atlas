Sally Johnston (née Dunbar) was the Officer in Charge of the original police
investigation into the death of John Russell at Bondi Police Station in 1989. She
was a junior plain clothes constable at the time.

The investigation proceeded on the assumption of misadventure, possibly due to
Russell's blood alcohol level. No persons of interest were identified.

In May 2023, the Sackar Inquiry contacted Sally Johnston in relation to her
involvement as OIC. Subsequent attempts to obtain a statement or submissions from
her were unsuccessful.
