Investigative piece on the near-total failure to prosecute hate crimes under Australian law.

## Key content

- Only 21 convictions under hate crime laws in Australia ever
- Zero convictions in NSW since records began in 1994
- Victorian police flagged 4,257 incidents over four years with only 3 convictions under the Racial and Religious Tolerance Act
- US expert Matt Browning: Australian police had *"heads in the sand"*
