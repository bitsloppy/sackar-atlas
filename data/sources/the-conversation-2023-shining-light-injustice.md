Written at/after the SCOI's final public sitting. Comprehensive overview of what the Inquiry achieved and found.

## Key content

- Sackar closing remarks: *"Many voiceless people have been given a voice"*
- **32 deaths examined** (key number — use in project description)
- 17 public + 48 private hearings
- 150,000+ documents compelled
- 130+ public submissions
- Mentions Strike Forces Parrabell, **Macnamir** (⚠️ not yet documented in project), and Neiwand
- Two named forensic breakthroughs:
  1. **Crispin Dye** — died Christmas Day 1993; clothing never forensically tested; DNA match to 2002 crime scene → unknown (now deceased) POI
  2. **Ernest Head** — Summer Hill, 1976; found naked, stabbed 35 times; palmprint re-analysis → known (now deceased) POI identified for first time
