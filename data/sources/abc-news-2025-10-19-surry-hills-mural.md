"Queer Sydney: A History" mural removed from Surry Hills Police Station office days after installation.

## Key content

- Mural by artist Jeremy Smith: depicted Magda Szubanski, Courtney Act, Michael Kirby, the first Mardi Gras, HIV/AIDS era, same-sex marriage
- Hung on an office wall at Surry Hills Police Station metres from Oxford Street
- Removed after an officer complained
- NSW Police statement: *"we need to ensure all officers are able to work in a professional and inclusive office"*
- Garry Wotherspoon quoted
- October 2025 — this is a **contemporary accountability event**, not historical
