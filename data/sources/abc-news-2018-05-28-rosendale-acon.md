⚠️ **Primary-source quality.** Contains eyewitness testimony not available elsewhere in the public record.

## Key content

- **Alan Rosendale's** testimony on his 1989 attack at Moore Park
- **Paul Simes** (eyewitness) — wrote down the attackers' number plate, which police later told him matched an **unmarked police car**
- Rosendale and Simes met for the first time in 2013, after Simes published his account
- Covers the ACON 2018 report *In Pursuit of Truth & Justice*

## Why primary-source-quality

The Simes eyewitness account — that the number plate matched an unmarked police car — is a direct allegation of police involvement in a gay hate attack. This goes beyond systemic failure and into active misconduct. Simes is a named, traceable witness who has published his account.

This source should be central to the Rosendale case file and flagged explicitly in the `police_misconduct_level` and `police_misconduct_summary` fields.

## Research notes

The Rosendale case is notable as a non-fatal assault documented in Report 58 by name. He survived; his case was part of the ACON 88. The Simes allegation makes it exceptional.
