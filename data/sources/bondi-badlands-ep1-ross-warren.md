Episode 1 of the *Bondi Badlands* podcast. Covers the disappearance of Ross Warren from Marks Park, Tamarama, in July 1989.

Ross Warren was a high-profile TV newsreader and weatherman. His disappearance received nationwide news coverage but was subject to a "shoddy" police investigation — no links were drawn at the time to a series of other murders at Bondi and across Sydney.
