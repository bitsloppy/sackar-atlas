**Bondi Badlands** is a five-part podcast series produced by The Sydney Morning Herald and The Age, created and presented by journalist Greg Callaghan. It is based on his 2007 book of the same name. The series investigates the murders and mysterious disappearances of gay men on the southern headland at Bondi Beach during the late 1980s and early 1990s.

Supported by ACON and Waverley Council. First episode released October 2021.

## Research notes

Episode 4 covers the coronial inquest in detail, including prison phone taps by police and the emergence of witnesses. The Deputy State Coroner Jacqueline Milledge condemned the original investigations as "lacklustre" and "shameful."

This podcast is a significant secondary source for the Marks Park/Bondi cliffs cases. Callaghan's 2007 book (which predates the SCOI) provides a baseline journalistic account; the podcast updates this with 2021 knowledge including the Scott Johnson arrest.
