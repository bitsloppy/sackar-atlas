LGBTQI+ collections, known publicly as the "Queerbrarian." Cooling manages and
promotes the State Library's LGBTQI+ holdings, which include CAMP NSW records,

In a January 2026 ABC Radio National *Sunday Extra* segment, Cooling discussed
the State Library's LGBTQI+ collections and the Australian Queer Archives (AQuA),
contextualising them within the SCOI findings and the broader urgency of preserving
queer history while witnesses are still living.
