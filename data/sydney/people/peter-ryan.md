Peter Ryan served as Commissioner of Police from August 1996 to April 2002, having been recruited from the United Kingdom to lead the reform of NSW Police in the wake of the Wood Royal Commission. His tenure was primarily focused on structural and cultural reform of the force following the Wood Commission findings.
