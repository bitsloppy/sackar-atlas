Leonard James Nash served as City Coroner in Sydney. He conducted the inquest into the death of David Lloyd-Williams, held on 23 October 1978. His finding was that Mr Lloyd-Williams had "cast himself from a cliff with the intention of taking his own life whilst in a state of mental depression."
