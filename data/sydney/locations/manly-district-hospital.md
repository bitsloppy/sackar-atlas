Stub — the former Manly District Hospital on Darley Road, Manly, received the body of Paul Rath on 16 June 1977, where he was pronounced dead after Police Rescue Squad recovered his body from the base of the Shelley Headland cliffs. The hospital served the northern beaches area for most of the period covered by this project and is likely to appear in other cases involving deaths in Manly and surrounds.

The hospital closed in 2018 when the Northern Beaches Hospital opened at Frenchs Forest.
