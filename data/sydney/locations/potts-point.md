Stub — Potts Point and the broader Kings Cross precinct was a significant centre of LGBTIQ life in Sydney from the 1960s through the 1980s. The Chevron Hotel on Macleay Street, whose downstairs bar (the Quarter Deck) was a well-known gathering place for gay men, is one of the most documented venues of this era in this project's scope.
