Covers the unveiling of the *Rise* memorial at Marks Park, Tamarama.

## Key content

- **Rise** memorial: designed by Urban Arts Projects; six-level stone terrace representing six bands of the pride flag
- Commissioned by Waverley Council
- Ross Warren's name engraved on the memorial
- Garry Wotherspoon: *"it could've been me, rather than the people I know"*
