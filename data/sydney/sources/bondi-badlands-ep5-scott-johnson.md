Episode 5 of the *Bondi Badlands* podcast. Covers the death of Scott Johnson at North Head, Manly, in December 1988, and his brother Steve Johnson's 32-year campaign for justice.

Note: Scott Johnson's case involves North Head (Manly/Gayamaygal Country) rather than the Bondi cliffs, making it geographically distinct from the other four cases in the series. Its inclusion connects the broader pattern of gay hate crimes across Sydney's headlands.

By the time this episode was released (October 2021), Scott White had been charged with murder (May 2020) following an informant tip. The episode draws the arc from the original 1988 death through three coronial inquests to the eventual arrest.
