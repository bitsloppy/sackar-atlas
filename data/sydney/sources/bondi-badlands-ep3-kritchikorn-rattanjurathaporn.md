Episode 3 of the *Bondi Badlands* podcast. Covers the murder of Kritchikorn Rattanjurathaporn, found below the Marks Park cliffs in 1990.

Rattanjurathaporn had only been in Australia for four months when he was brutally murdered at Mackenzies Point. His case is significant for the project's CALD dimension — a young Thai man murdered in a country he had only just arrived in.
