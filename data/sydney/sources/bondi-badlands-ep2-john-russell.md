Episode 2 of the *Bondi Badlands* podcast. Covers the murder of John Russell, found below the Bondi cliffs in November 1989.

Russell was about to leave Sydney behind for an idyllic life in regional New South Wales. He became one of the victims of Sydney's notorious gay hate crimes.
