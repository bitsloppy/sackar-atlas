Stub — Moore Park is a large public reserve adjacent to Centennial Park and the
SCG in the inner south of Sydney. The park and its surrounds were a known beat
in the 1980s.

The area is documented in the case of **Alan Rosendale**, who was attacked at
Moore Park in 1989 and survived. His case is documented in ACON's *In Pursuit of
Truth & Justice* (2018) and named in *Report 58* (NSW Legislative Council, 2021).
The ABC News article by Alan Rosendale (28 May 2018) is primary-source-quality:
it includes eyewitness testimony from Paul Simes, who recorded the attackers'
vehicle registration and later found it matched an unmarked police car.
