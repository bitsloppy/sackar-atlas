## Two modes

**Archival** — Historical newspaper coverage sourced via Trove and other archives.
Links back to the open dataset. Contextualises what the data shows.

**Original** — New writing by Anna Roberts. Analysis, essays, profiles.
Connects the historical record to contemporary significance.

---

## Format

Articles are markdown files. Frontmatter includes:
- `title`
- `date`
- `type` (archival | original)
- `related_cases` (links to case records in `data/`)
- `sources`
